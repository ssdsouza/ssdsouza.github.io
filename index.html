Who we are?

We are Team "Go Trail Ranger" from USC Viterbi School of Engineering.

What we did in this project?

Developed Go Trail Ranger which is an obstacle-avoiding computer game, where the bot runs on a path and collects boosters and coins while its score is continuously increasing as it runs forward. The main objective of the bot is to collect as many coins as possible without bumping into any of the stationary or moving obstacles.

What tools are we using?

Unity Game Engine

PyAutoGUI and PyTesseract

PyTorch

Google Colab

PyCharm Editor
